Jalebi
Ingredients
  • 1⁄2 tin Nestle Milkmaid
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 600gm Paneer
  • 200gm Sooji
  • 1 1⁄2 tsp Baking Powder
  • Oil
  • 2 litres Water
  • 1kg Sugar
  • 6-8 Cardamom
How to make?
  1. Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup remove from fire; add cardamom and keep aside to cool.
  2. In a mixing bowl, put the flour, paneer, sooji, nestle milkmaid, baking powder and baking soda. Mix gently to make a soft dough, do not knead too much.
  3. Divide the mixture into 30-35 portions and gently roll into round gulab jamuns. Fry in the oil at very low flame till golden brown in colour.
  4. Keep putting the fried gulab jamuns in the prepared sugar syrup,once all the gulab jamuns are in the sugar syrup bring it to boul and remove from flame.
  5. Serve warm.
